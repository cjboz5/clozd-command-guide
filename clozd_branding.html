Getting started

Three ways to trigger Clozd branding

The applying-clozd-design-system skill can be launched from the plugin menu, a slash command, or by simply asking Claude in plain English. Pick the approach that feels most natural.

Plugin path — step by step
1

Open Claude desktop and click Cowork.

2

Click the + button in the chat box.

3

Hover over Plugins.

4

Select applying-clozd-design-system from the list.

5

Describe what you want Claude to build or brand.

Two faster ways to trigger it
  • Slash command — Type /applying-clozd-design-system in the chat box, then give Claude your content or context.
  • Natural language — Just describe what you want and mention Clozd branding in your prompt. Claude recognizes the request and applies the design system automatically.

What "Clozd-branded" actually means
The design standards Claude applies

When you request Clozd branding, Claude applies a complete system — not just colors. Every branded output follows these standards:

What gets applied
Colors: Patina (#09D4C8), Blue (#14A6F8), Royal Blue (#274FDB) — gradient order strictly maintained Typography: Inter font family with defined heading hierarchy (48pt, 36pt, 24pt, 16pt) Layout: One idea per slide, generous whitespace, minimal bullets, strong visual hierarchy Voice: Authentic, direct, confident, solution-focused — no unnecessary jargon

Getting the most out of Claude
Tips for best results
A few simple habits make your branded outputs sharper, more consistent, and easier to use.
1

You don't need the slash command

Just say "make it Clozd-branded" or "apply Clozd branding" anywhere in your prompt. Claude recognizes design intent in plain English and triggers the skill automatically.

2

Specify the output type

Claude produces sharper results when you say what you want — a deck, a report, a one-pager, a landing page. "Make this a Clozd-branded slide deck" gives Claude more to work with than just "make it look nice."

3

Sales plugin skills already handle branding

Skills like profiling-prospects and researching-executive-insights apply Clozd branding automatically when they produce a deliverable. You only need to invoke the design skill separately if you're starting from scratch or refining raw content.

4

One conversation, start to finish

Research, write, refine, and brand — all without starting over. Claude retains context across the session, so your final branded output reflects everything that came before it.
