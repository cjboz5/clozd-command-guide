Getting started

How to access Clozd workflows

Open the Claude desktop app and click Cowork in the top nav. From there, launch any Clozd Sales workflow in seconds — via the plugin menu, a slash command, or plain English.

Plugin path — step by step
1

Open Claude desktop and click Cowork.

2

Click the + button in the chat box.

3

Hover over Plugins.

4

Hover over Clozd Sales.

5

Select the workflow you want to run.

Three ways to launch any workflow
  • Plugin menu — Open the Clozd Sales plugin in Cowork and pick your workflow.
  • Slash commands — Type / in the chat box and select from the list.
  • Natural language — Describe what you need in plain English. Claude matches the right workflow automatically.
Try prompts like
Research Gong before my meeting Prep me for my call with Adobe Build a Clozd-branded deck

Power moves
Combine workflows for better output
The best results come from chaining workflows together. Start with research or prep, then turn that output into strategy, outreach, or a polished deliverable — all in one conversation.

Account prep

Research the account, prep the meeting, then draft follow-up outreach.

account-research → call-prep → draft-outreach
"Research Datadog" → "Now prep me for my call with them" → "Draft follow-up outreach based on that prep."

Executive selling

Build a point of view, turn it into an asset, then brand it for Clozd.

researching-executive-insights → create-an-asset → applying-clozd-design-system

Strategic proposal

Build a proposal recommendation, then package it into a customer-ready deliverable.

profiling-prospects → create-an-asset → applying-clozd-design-system

Getting the most out of Claude
Tips for best results
A few simple habits make the outputs sharper, more relevant, and easier to use in real sales situations.
1

Lead with the job to be done

Say what you want clearly — research the account, prep the meeting, coach the deal, or build the asset. Claude will pick the right workflow.

2

Natural language is fine

Use the plugin, a slash command, or plain English. Claude doesn't need perfect syntax — describe what you need and it figures it out.

3

Add context when it matters

A bit of extra detail about the account, audience, deal stage, or competitor usually makes the output significantly sharper.

4

Chain workflows together

Research can become prep. Prep can become outreach. Insight can become a polished, Clozd-branded deliverable — all in one session.
